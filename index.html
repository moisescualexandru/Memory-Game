Instructions

In order to play press the 'Start Game' button.

In order to restart the game press the 'Reset Grid' button and the 'Start Game'.

You need to match the cards on the grid by turning them one by one. A match will be completed if you find the same card on the second turn. Otherwise the 2 cards will unturn.

Try and complete the hole grid with as few moves as possible.

Have fun!!
